Model of Great Grey Wolf Sif from Dark Souls

Dec. 2024

I made this as a Secret Santa gift for my friend. The figure itself is from Thingiverse, and I made the base. The base is filled with powdered lead to keep the figure upright. I 3D printed it, then painted it. The eyes were particularly fun, as I had to use the tip of a knife to paint the pupils.

[Unfinished] Wooden Master Sword

Dec. 2024

This was my first attempt at making a Secret Santa gift for my friend, but I didn't have the time to do what I wanted to do with it. Here's what I threw together one late night, while blasting classic rock on the radio. It's about 4 ft. long. It was my first time working with wood beyond hammering nails into boards, and I think it's pretty good so far. Most imporantly, it's sturdy, and I can swing it around without issues. I think it would fail when it comes to actually hitting things, but that's not the point. I ought to get back into this project.
